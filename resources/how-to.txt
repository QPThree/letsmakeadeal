How to Play

Inspired by the Classic Game Show "Let's Make A Deal!"

1. The User will choose to start a game and will be awarded an initial winnings.
2. Each turn, the User will be given one of two options: walk away with their current winnings,
   or risk it all to gain an additional prize.
3. If the user unboxes a "Zonk" prize, then they lose all of their winnings!
4. What prizes await!? How much are you willing to risk!?
